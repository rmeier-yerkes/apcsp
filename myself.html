My Page About Myself

My hobbies include writing code (just in Python for now, though I am learning Java), origami, poetry, and reading. My favorite television show is Forged in Fire and my favorite movie is 1917. I don't listen to music much; my musical taste is probably best described as eclectic and antiquarian: I enjoy songs from a large variety of genres, but especially older-to-ancient music and Gregorian chant.
